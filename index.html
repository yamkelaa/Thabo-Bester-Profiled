THE BIRTH OF THABO BESTER

Thabo Bester was born to Maria Mabaso on the 13th of June 1986 at Chris Hani Baragwanath Hospital in Soweto. She was only 16 at the time and this was her first child, in various interviews she details how Thabo was conceived through rape and because this was such a traumatic experience for her she was understandably unable to identify his father and reluctant to look for him, however members of her community allege he is a local Portugese Shopkeeper who bears a striking resemblence to Thabo.

ABANDONED BY HIS MOTHER?

Upon his birth a young Maria did take care of Thabo until he was a about a year old upon which she left him in the care of her mother, Thabos grandmother in order to go and find work. During this time she also considered giving Thabo up for adoption, as an Indian family form Lenasia had asked to raise him, but she decided against it as she felt sure he was going to be a good child.
Upon being given to his grandmother Johanna Bester, Thabo was raised in a small farming community called Eikenhof. According to Thabo his childhood was extremely difficult, he felt abandoned by his mother and described being sexually abused mutliple times, first by a man his grandmother was seeing, and second by a stranger who had offered him shelter when he had run away from home.

17-year-old Bester, released into the care of his mother after a short stint in prison for theft. He alleges he also was sexually abused by gang members during this time.

YOUNG CRIMINAL

Thabo Bester attended Laerskool Danie Theron situated in the South of Johannesburg from 1997 at the age of eleven, before eventually dropping out during the third term of his seventh grade. It was then that he began his criminal career, he would pretend to be a destitute high school student in need of shelter and a home and would go on to receive food, clothes and even a cellphone from his unsuspecting victims, one of whom later spotted him in a mall appearing well off, and dressed to the nines, a far contradiction from the boy who they had previously picked up after seeing him stand by the side of the road asking for a lift home. FACEBOOK RAPIST

Upon his release, a young, unfazed Bester who according to South African Home Affairs Minister Dr. Aaron Motsoaledi could only be identified by his criminal record as he had no birth certificate began building a criminal enterpise, setting up a facebook page where he pretended to be a well-respected scout, with a host of international clients who needed up and coming models. He would later communicate to young women on the platform, offer them a photoshoot and proceed to rape and rob them upon their arrival. In other instances he would book hotels and professional photgraphers, with the assistance of fake proof of payments, orchastrate elaborate shoots and whilst the models and photographers were at lunch tafeoff with their personal belonings and professinal equipment.

CONVICTED MURDERER

In a crime that sent shockwaves across the nation, and led to the arrest of Bester who at the time was already on the run for his rape and robberies, 26-year-old Nomfundo Tyhulu who was in a relationship with Thabo at the time was stabbed four times at a Cape Town guesthouse where she and Bester were staying at for a short vacation. Upon stabbing her, he confessed to asking her for her laptop password as she lay dying, later telling hotel staff to not wake her up, before making off with her belongings. He was later given a life sentence for this crime, in addition to 50 years for his rape and robberies.

26-Year-Old Nomfundo Tyhulu. She was brutally murdered by Bester, who demanded her laptop password as she lay dying.

SPOTTED

In a shocking turn of events, Thabo Bester, who was believed to have died by suicide according to the Department of Correctional Services, was spotted at Sandton City alongside Dr. Nandipha Magudumana, a well-known doctor and aesthetician, and her two children. This sighting, which occurred approximately a year after Bester's reported death, caused a global sensation as the image circulated on Twitter. The unexpected reappearance of Bester and his association with Dr. Magudumana sparked widespread speculation and curiosity. Shortly after the sighting in question, the duo went on the run, leaving behind their luxurious Hyde Park residence, dropping off Magudumanas children at school and taking refuge in a motel before crossinf the border. However, their escape was short-lived as they were eventually apprehended in Tanzania, thanks to a joint effort by law enforcement agencies. The subsequent return to South Africa under heavy police guard and the legal proceedings thereafter only added to the intrigue surrounding this extraordinary case. Bester was transported to Kgosi Mampuru C-Max Prison while Dr. Magudumana was remanded in police custody and faced charges in the Bloemfontein Magistrates Court on the same day.

A BRAZEN ESCAPE

Upon their apprehension, shocking details about Bester's escape and Magudumana's assistance in this regard began to emerge, causing international uproar, parliamentary debates, and an irreversible breakdown in the relationship between the Department of Correctional Services and G4S, the company hired to manage the prison from which Bester had escaped. Eyewitness accounts, confessions from accomplices, and a daring statement from Bester's former cell-mate painted a vivid picture of an audacious escpae. Prior to his alleged suicide, Bester had shared with fellow inmates that he was in high spirits as he was soon to leave. He was later found burnt beyond recognition in his jail cell after an explosion had occurred. It was later revealed that he had in fact, hired prison guards to place a dead body in his cell, start a fire ,pretend not to notice until it was too late, disable CCTV cameras, and walk him out of the prison in G4S uniform into the welcoming arms of his girlfriend, Dr. Nandipha Magudumana.

MORE VICTIMS?

DNA tests later revealed that the body found burnt beyond recognition in Besters cell, was that of 31-year-old Katlego Bereng. Authorities were further puzzled when it was revealed that Bereng had actually passed on from blunt force trauma to the head. What added to the intrigue was the fact that he was acquainted with one of the accomplices involved in Besters' brazen escape just two months prior to his disappearance in March 2022. Sadly, more victimes are said to have fallen prey to Bester and Magudumans elaborate schemes, the helper and gardener who had been employed at the residence where Magudumana and Bester resided shelter after his escape seemed to vanish into thin air. Their families grew increasingly frantic in their search for them, but they were never found. Police have strong suspicions that they may have been buried in the yard of the Hyde Park mansion where they worked, a chilling possibility that only deepens the mystery surrounding this perplexing case.

THE GOOD DOCTOR

Perhaps one of the most shocking aspects within the Thabo Bester saga is the involvement of famed medical doctor and CEO of a popular aesthetics suite, Dr. Nandipha Magudumana. The world watched in shock as her involvement, which included regularly seeing Bester in prison allegedly for conjugal visits while she was married, organizing multiple dead bodies to facilitate false insurance claims, and insisting Bester was her husband and that she wanted his body cremated immediately after his death, were laid bare. Questions began to arise: Why would a young, successful doctor married to another doctor with two girls risk it all for a convicted murderer and primary school dropout?

'23 BONNIE AND CLYDE

Magudumana was born in a small farming communitiy within the Eastern Cape known as Bizana , in 1983. However she grew up in KwaZulu Natal, Port Edward, a small town situated on the South Coast. It was then there that she discovered her passion for medicine and health. After highschool she went off to Johannsburg and pursued her BSc General at the University of The Witwatersand, before obtaing her medical degree.
It is alleged that Bester and Mgudumana were first involved whilst she was in university and they they later broke things off. However durng his time in prison, Bester reached out to Magudumana, legedly buying her gifts and sending money obtained from his scams. Enitced, the popular doctor begaan a romantic relationship with the murderer that continued even after his escpae, as she and her young girls resided with him fugitive in the infamous Hyde Park hideout.
